What Should Learners Know about Ed-Tech?


In many ways, the resources listed under both the "Technologists" and "Teachers" section of this guide are useful for learners. That is, it's worth considering how ed-tech views students just as its worth thinking about how education views students.

Questions students should ask about the ed-tech they're compelled to use include:

  • Who owns my data?
  • Who has access to my data? Who is it shared with
  • How long is my data stored?
